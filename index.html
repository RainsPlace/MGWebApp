🧮 How Points Are Calculated
The Formula
Points = ⌈ 1000 × √N ÷ √P × (1 + log₁₀(B)) ⌉
Where N = total players, P = finishing position, and B = buy-in amount. The ⌈ ⌉ symbol means the result is always rounded up to the nearest whole number.
Field Size Effect
Every position earns more points when more players enter. Beating 100 players pays more than beating 10 — the √N factor scales the entire payout table up as fields grow.
Position Decay
Points drop with each finishing position following a square-root curve. The gap between 1st and 2nd is large; the gap between 50th and 51st is small. Every place that finishes earns points — there is no cut-off.
Team Scoring — Top 5 Rule
Each team has multiple players competing each game. Only the top 5 individual scores from each team count toward that team's game total. This rewards depth — teams need consistent performers, not just one star player.
No Bonus Points
This league uses pure finish-position points only. There are no bounty bonuses, streak bonuses, or attendance bonuses. Your score is exactly what the formula produces.
Quarterly Leaderboard
The leaderboard runs for one full quarter. All game scores accumulate over the quarter and are tracked both here on this webapp and on the main league leaderboard. At the end of the quarter, the top team is crowned champion. Individual performances are tracked throughout and each player's stats are monitored for yearly achievement, giving every player a record of their advancement over time.
